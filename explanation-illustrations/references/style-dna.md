# Style DNA

## In one sentence

Pure white, minimalist, hand-drawn, lots of white space, restrained, absurd, product-sketch feeling, handwritten feel, structure that is clear but not an instruction manual.

Like someone who has spent years doing AI, product, design, and developer tools casually sketching an explanatory drawing on a blank sheet of paper.

## Must have

- 16:9 landscape article illustration.
- Pure white background: no beige, warm gray, paper texture, gradient, shadow, noise, or vintage-paper look.
- Mostly black hand-drawn line art: thin lines, slightly wobbly, not mechanical, not vector, not heavy outlines.
- Lots of white space: the subject occupies about 40%–60% of the canvas, at least 35% blank, ideally one whole quiet area.
- A few handwritten **English** annotations: at most 5–8 of them, each ideally 1–4 short words.
- One image tells only one core action, structure, state, or metaphor.
- Express the structure naturally — do not write the name of the structure type on the image.

## Color

- Black: main line art, character, frames, structure, primary text, main objects.
- Red: key annotations, problems, emotional beats, critical reminders, results.
- Orange: main flow, paths, arrows, automation flow, the A-to-B movement relationship.
- Blue: supplementary notes, mental state, system state, second-layer explanation, AI/assistant/automation hints.

Blue is not required in every image. Keep color restrained — when in doubt, use less.

## Absolutely not

- No commercial illustration.
- No PPT infographic.
- No formal flowchart.
- No course slides.
- No cute cartoon poster.
- No children's illustration.
- No complex architecture diagram.
- No polished flat illustration.
- No techy UI look.
- No realistic app screenshot.
- No complex background, gradient, shadow, or texture.
- Do not explain every node.
- No top-left type-title like "Workflow / System Architecture / Common Pitfalls / Roadmap".

## Aesthetic direction

It should be absurd, creative, interesting, clean, and imaginative.

It should not be cute, childish, overly complex, or stiff.
